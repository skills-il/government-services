# Domain Checklist: israeli-bituach-leumi

Anchor for Expert Review. Covers National Insurance contributions AND benefits.

## Must cover (core)
- Contribution rates by status: employee (4.27%/12.17%), employer (4.51%/7.6%), self-employed (7.7%/18%) -- btl.gov.il rates pages.
- Non-working residents and non-work (passive) income: 266 minimum, 3,442 exemption, 6.92%/7% NI + 5.17% health -- btl.gov.il NotWorking + non-working rates.
- Contribution EXEMPTIONS (override the rate tables): non-working spouse / akeret bayit = 0 (Form 673); old-age pension recipients / retirement age = NI-portion exempt, 688 NIS non-work-income floor; disability 75%+/100% permanent = exempt -- btl.gov.il "מי פטור" + kolzchut.
- Coordination: passive-income exemption does NOT stack on work income.
- The 13 main benefit programs with 2026 amounts and qualifying periods -- references/benefit-programs.md.
- Claim forms per benefit (480, 1500, 355, 7801, etc.) -- references/forms.md.

## Should cover (advanced)
- New-immigrant (oleh) contribution reductions in the early residency window.
- Residents working abroad for a foreign employer (bilateral SS treaty coordination, forms 627/628).
- Working-pensioner reduced NI rate.

## Out of scope (explicit)
- Under-18 separate liability (covered via household, not separately liable).
- Private insurance / kupat cholim membership questions (different domain).
- Detailed appeal litigation procedure (the skill points to the labor court, full procedure is legal-tech scope).

## Authoritative sources
- btl.gov.il (rates, NotWorking, "מי פטור מתשלום דמי ביטוח לאומי", benefit pages, 2026 rate circular)
- kolzchut.org.il (המוסד לביטוח לאומי hub, פטור מתשלום דמי ביטוח לאומי)
- *6050 BTL phone
