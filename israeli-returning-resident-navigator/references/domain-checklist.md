# Domain Checklist: Israeli Returning Resident Navigator

Scope: end-to-end process navigator for Israelis returning to Israel after years abroad. Helps disambiguate eligibility tracks (regular toshav chozer / toshav chozer vatik / shorter Misrad HaAliyah track) and walks through the first 90 days of re-entry. Process-level only, not tax math and not customs/vehicle.

Last research date: 2026-05-23.

## Must cover (core process - agent must surface these in any meaningful answer)

1. **Three definitions are different and live with different agencies**
   - Misrad HaAliyah V'HaKlita: "תושב חוזר" = Israeli citizen, age 17+, with at least **2 years** of residence abroad, who did not visit Israel for more than 4 months in any year of the last 2 years before return ([gov.il - Who is a returning resident](https://www.gov.il/en/pages/returning_residents_whois), updated 27.10.2024). This is the basis for the "תעודת תושב חוזר" certificate that unlocks ministry assistance.
   - Mas Hachnasa (Income Tax Authority): "תושב חוזר רגיל" = at least **6 consecutive years** as a foreign tax resident. "תושב חוזר ותיק" = at least **10 consecutive years** abroad (a temporary Hora'at Sha'ah 6-year-equals-vatik track existed for returns through end-of-2009 and was not extended; confirm exact dates at answer time) ([kolzchut - returning resident](https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91_%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8)).
   - Bituach Leumi: residency is decided independently via Form 628 (שאלון לקביעת תושבות), based on center of life, not on calendar years ([btl.gov.il - toshav chozer](https://www.btl.gov.il/Audience/toshavChozer/Pages/default.aspx)).
   - Agents that conflate these three tracks give wrong advice. The skill must explicitly disambiguate which question lives at which agency.

2. **Misrad HaAliyah registration process**
   - Service: "בדיקת זכאות לקבלת מעמד תושב חוזר" - online form at [gov.il application page](https://www.gov.il/he/service/application_for_recognition_as_returning_resident) (updated 24.07.2025).
   - Outcome: "תעודת תושב חוזר" (returning resident certificate) issued by the local Misrad HaAliyah office near the applicant's residence in Israel. Office finder: [gov.il Misrad HaAliyah branches](https://www.gov.il/he/government-service-branches/?officeId=27db3169-ab0e-490c-af70-6d03133cb1f3).
   - Proof required: passport entry/exit stamps, Ministry of Interior records (gov.il source above).
   - Misrad HaAliyah service phone: \*2994 or 03-9733333 (gov.il application page).

3. **Eligibility windows**
   - Ministry assistance: **24 months** from date of return (assured income narrower at 12 months) ([gov.il Who is a returning resident](https://www.gov.il/en/pages/returning_residents_whois)).
   - Customs/vehicle window: **9 months** from date of return (see `israeli-returning-resident-customs-vehicle` for the full mechanics; this skill only mentions the deadline).
   - Mas Hachnasa benefits: **10-year** exemption window for toshav chozer vatik begins on the date of becoming an Israeli tax resident again ([kolzchut - 10 years abroad benefits](https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%94%D7%98%D7%91%D7%95%D7%AA_%D7%91%D7%9E%D7%A1_%D7%9C%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%A9%D7%A9%D7%94%D7%95_%D7%91%D7%97%D7%95%22%D7%9C_%D7%9E%D7%A2%D7%9C_10_%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%9D)).

4. **Bituach Leumi re-classification**
   - Submit Form 628 ("שאלון לקביעת תושבות לתושב חוזר") at a BL branch or via the online personal area ([kolzchut - dmei BL for returning residents](https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%93%D7%9E%D7%99_%D7%91%D7%99%D7%98%D7%95%D7%97_%D7%9C%D7%90%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%99_%D7%9C%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D), updated 03.03.2025).
   - BL phone for returnees: \*6050 (Israel) or +972-8-936-9669 (from abroad, Sun-Thu 8:00-15:00) ([btl.gov.il - toshav chozer](https://www.btl.gov.il/Audience/toshavChozer/Pages/default.aspx)).
   - Benefits NOT available upon return: maternity allowance, unemployment (dmei avtala), employer insolvency. These require accumulated work periods after re-classification (kolzchut dmei BL page).
   - Anyone who didn't pay BL while abroad: BL may demand back-payment to maintain insurance continuity ("רצף ביטוחי"); confirm exposure with the BL branch before submitting Form 628.

5. **Kupat cholim re-enrollment + waiting period**
   - Waiting period: **1 month per year of absence**, minimum **2 months (חודשיים)** for those abroad 12+ months, maximum **6 months** ([kolzchut - joining kupat cholim as returning resident](https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%94%D7%A6%D7%98%D7%A8%D7%A4%D7%95%D7%AA_%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91_%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8_%D7%9C%D7%91%D7%99%D7%98%D7%95%D7%97_%D7%91%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%90%D7%95%D7%AA), updated 15.01.2026).
   - "Year of absence" = 12-month period with 183+ days outside Israel (kolzchut page above).
   - "Waiting month" = 25 consecutive days of residence in Israel; trips abroad shorter than 25 days don't count toward completing the waiting period (kolzchut page above).
   - Pidyon (waiting-period redemption): **16,860 NIS in 2026**, one-time or up to 6 equal installments (kolzchut page above).
   - During waiting period: returnee still pays health insurance premiums based on income (kolzchut page above).
   - Choice of kupah (Clalit / Maccabi / Meuhedet / Leumit) is made on the BL registration form together with residency determination.

6. **Driver's license restoration**
   - Israeli license that expired while abroad: can be restored without a driving test if the returnee proves driving abroad ([kolzchut - converting foreign license, returnee section](https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%A8%D7%AA_%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99%D7%95%D7%9F_%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%99%D7%92%D7%94_%D7%96%D7%A8_%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99%D7%95%D7%9F_%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%99%D7%92%D7%94_%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%A8%D7%90%D7%9C%D7%99)).
   - Conversion of a foreign license to Israeli: window is up to **5 years from the date of return**; the foreign license must be a permanent license issued at least 6 months before entering Israel AND the holder must have at least 5 years of driving experience on it ([kolzchut - converting foreign license](https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%A8%D7%AA_%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99%D7%95%D7%9F_%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%99%D7%92%D7%94_%D7%96%D7%A8_%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99%D7%95%D7%9F_%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%99%D7%92%D7%94_%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%A8%D7%90%D7%9C%D7%99)).

7. **Family - children and Hebrew**
   - Returnee children may qualify for partial gan/preschool fee assistance in the first year back; specifics depend on the locality, so check with the municipality's חינוך department on arrival (kolzchut returning residents landing page).
   - Digital Hebrew learning resources from Misrad HaAliyah for returnees and their children; the service center (\*2994) can point users to current program names (kolzchut returning residents landing page).
   - **Returnees do NOT receive Sal Klita** like olim chadashim. The Misrad HaAliyah benefit basket for returnees is partial absorption support, not the full immigrant grant (kolzchut returning residents landing page - only oleh chadash / oleh-chozer combined statuses get the full basket).
   - **Children born abroad** to Israeli-citizen parents must be registered with Misrad HaPnim (רישום לידת חוץ) BEFORE BL or kupat cholim can open a residency case for them. Confirm current form at gov.il at answer time.
   - **Meitav (IDF) coordination** for returnees aged 17-28 who left as minors: unresolved IDF obligations can block license issuance and other services. Send them to the local Meitav office before they hit Misrad HaRishui.

8. **Hard boundaries - Do NOT use for**
   - Olim chadashim under Chok HaShvut - use `israeli-aliyah-navigator`.
   - Israelis still abroad planning to leave - use `israeli-relocation-abroad`.
   - Section 14 tax mechanics, Form 1348 drafting, 10-year exemption math - use `israeli-toshav-chozer-vatik-tax-planner`.
   - Vehicle import quotas, household shipment caps, Meches Section 17 - use `israeli-returning-resident-customs-vehicle`.
   - Binding tax or legal advice. The skill is a process-navigation aid; numeric tax calculations and legal opinions must be handed off to a CPA / מייצג / lawyer.

## Should cover (high-value but not blocking)

- **Order of operations checklist for first 90 days**: airport entry → Misrad HaPnim address update → Misrad HaAliyah eligibility check → BL Form 628 → kupat cholim selection → driver license office → school/gan enrollment → bank account reopening.
- **Pension and keren hishtalmut**: existing funds remain; if the returnee was insured under a kollektivi/employer policy that lapsed, reactivation rules differ by fund. Note that anyone with 12+ consecutive months without pension insurance may need to re-enroll formally ([nevo - pension law](https://www.nevo.co.il/law_html/law00/84255.htm) general reference; for specifics, refer the user to the fund's customer service).
- **Old-age pension (kiztavat zikna)** continuity rules - cross-reference `israeli-elder-care-navigator` if the returnee is age 60+.
- **Lone returnee (returnee from a "preferred country"/ארץ מועדפת)**: shorter eligibility tracks exist by year; verify against the current Misrad HaAliyah year list before quoting (gov.il subjects/immigration_and_absorption landing).
- **Retroactive benefits**: certain benefits may be requested up to 15 months after receiving toshav chozer status ([Nefesh B'Nefesh - benefits for toshavim chozrim](https://www.nbn.org.il/life-in-israel/government-services/rights-and-benefits/benefits-for-toshavim-chozrim-returning-residents/)).
- **Mas Hachnasa reporting change (2026)**: starting 01.01.2026 toshav chozer vatik must report all foreign assets and income to the Tax Authority even though the income remains tax-exempt ([bshcpa - toshav chozer vatik 2026](https://www.bshcpa.co.il/%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91-%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8-%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%A7/)). Surface as a one-line callout; the math lives in the sister tax skill.

## Out of scope (explicit non-goals)

- Customs household-goods caps, vehicle import quotas, 48-month vehicle age rule, Meches forms - sister skill `israeli-returning-resident-customs-vehicle`.
- Section 14 mechanics, Form 1348, ten-year exemption calculations, foreign-trust rules - sister skill `israeli-toshav-chozer-vatik-tax-planner`.
- Full sal klita grant calculation - that's for olim chadashim (handled by `israeli-aliyah-navigator`).
- Exit planning (Israelis leaving Israel) - handled by `israeli-relocation-abroad`.
- Specific kupat cholim plan recommendations / supplementary insurance comparison - handled by `israeli-hmo-navigator`.
- Legal advice on Israeli citizenship / passport renewal.
