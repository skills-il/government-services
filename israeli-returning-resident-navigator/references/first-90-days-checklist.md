# First 90 Days Checklist

A practical re-entry ordering. Some items have time-limited windows that close fast (customs = 9 months; Misrad HaAliyah eligibility = 24 months; foreign-license conversion = 5 years).

## Week 1 (days 1-7)

1. **Address update at Misrad HaPnim (Population Authority)** - required before BL recognises a new Israeli address. Walk-in or pre-booked appointment at any Lishkat HaRashut HaOchlosin.
2. **Reopen / verify Israeli bank account** - needed for any government deposit (BL benefits, ministry assistance). Some banks may have flagged the account as foreign-resident.
3. **Apply for "תעודת תושב חוזר" at Misrad HaAliyah V'HaKlita** - online form: https://www.gov.il/he/service/application_for_recognition_as_returning_resident . The certificate is the gate-key for ministry benefits and unlocks the customs window. Phone \*2994.

## Weeks 2-4

4. **File Form 628 at Bituach Leumi** ("שאלון לקביעת תושבות לתושב חוזר"). Bring passport with entry/exit stamps, foreign tax returns or work permits, lease/mortgage to show center of life. BL phone \*6050. Source: https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%93%D7%9E%D7%99_%D7%91%D7%99%D7%98%D7%95%D7%97_%D7%9C%D7%90%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%99_%D7%9C%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D .
5. **Choose kupat cholim on the BL registration form** - Clalit, Maccabi, Meuhedet, or Leumit. The choice triggers the waiting-period clock.
6. **Decide: pay pidyon (16,860 NIS in 2026) or wait through the waiting period?** Source: https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%94%D7%A6%D7%98%D7%A8%D7%A4%D7%95%D7%AA_%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%A9%D7%91_%D7%97%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8_%D7%9C%D7%91%D7%99%D7%98%D7%95%D7%97_%D7%91%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%90%D7%95%D7%AA . Even during the waiting period the returnee pays health premiums based on income.

## Month 2

7. **Driver's license**
   - If Israeli license expired abroad: book Misrad HaRishui (Licensing Bureau) appointment with proof of driving abroad (foreign license, insurance records) - usually no driving test required.
   - If only a foreign license: start the conversion process. Window is 5 years from return; the foreign license must be a permanent license issued ≥ 6 months before return AND the holder must have at least 5 years of driving experience on it.
   - Source: https://www.kolzchut.org.il/he/%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%A8%D7%AA_%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99%D7%95%D7%9F_%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%99%D7%92%D7%94_%D7%96%D7%A8_%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99%D7%95%D7%9F_%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%99%D7%92%D7%94_%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%A8%D7%90%D7%9C%D7%99 .
   - For returnees aged 17-28 who left as minors: clear Meitav (IDF recruitment) status BEFORE Misrad HaRishui; an open obligation can block license issuance.
8. **Children - school / gan enrollment** at the local municipality. Returnee children may qualify for partial gan/preschool fee assistance in the first year back; check with the municipality's חינוך department.
   - **Children born abroad**: register with Misrad HaPnim (רישום לידת חוץ) BEFORE BL or kupat cholim can open a residency case for them.
9. **Hebrew for children / spouse**: use the Misrad HaAliyah digital Hebrew resources or a local ulpan. Returnee adults do not get the full oleh-chadash sal-klita ulpan grant - coverage is partial.

## Month 3

10. **Pension and keren hishtalmut review**
    - Contact the existing fund's customer service. Re-activation rules depend on the length of the contribution gap and are fund-specific; let the fund tell you the threshold and whether a fresh enrolment is needed.
    - Coordinate with new employer: ensure new pension deposits land in the same fund to maintain seniority where the rules allow.
11. **Mas Hachnasa registration as Israeli tax resident (if not already)**. Open a tik mas / tik nikuim if self-employed or with foreign income. *Do not negotiate Section 14 / "vatik" status without a CPA* - hand the user off to `israeli-toshav-chozer-vatik-tax-planner` for the tax mechanics.
12. **Customs deadline check** - last call: the 9-month customs benefit window closes from the date of return. If shipping containers or importing a vehicle, push to `israeli-returning-resident-customs-vehicle` and act before month 9.

## Anytime in the 24-month window

- Use the Misrad HaAliyah eligibility window (24 months from return; 12 months for assured income) for employment counselling, professional retraining funding, scientist absorption, and small-business consulting.
- Retroactive benefits: some can be claimed up to 15 months after receiving toshav chozer status (https://www.nbn.org.il/life-in-israel/government-services/rights-and-benefits/benefits-for-toshavim-chozrim-returning-residents/).

## Skip if not applicable

- IDF reservist re-registration - relevant for men under reserve age; not covered here.
- Voter registration - Israeli citizens vote based on Misrad HaPnim address; the step is automatic after address update.
