# Israeli Government Bodies Guide

A comprehensive reference for identifying government bodies by their letterhead, reference number patterns, and common document types. Use this when you receive a document and need to figure out who sent it and what they want.

## Tax Authority (רשות המסים בישראל)

**Also known as:** Reshut HaMisim, Israel Tax Authority, Income Tax Authority (מס הכנסה), VAT Authority (מע"מ), Customs (מכס)

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- "רשות המסים בישראל" with the Finance Ministry logo
- May specify division: "אגף מס הכנסה", "אגף המכס ומע"מ"
- Regional office name (e.g., "פקיד שומה תל אביב 1")

**Reference number patterns:**
- Income tax file: 9-digit number (e.g., "תיק 123456789")
- VAT file: "מע"מ" + dealer number
- Assessment number: "שומה מס'" + sequential number

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Tax assessment | שומה | Their calculation of what you owe | HIGH - 30 days to object |
| Best judgment assessment | שומה לפי מיטב השפיטה | They calculated without your input (you did not file or they reject your filing) | HIGH - 30 days to object |
| Agreed assessment | שומה בהסכמה | You and the assessor agreed on the amount | LOW - already agreed |
| Objection decision | החלטה בהשגה | Their response to your objection | HIGH - 30 days to appeal to court |
| Refund notice | הודעה על החזר | You are getting money back | LOW - informational |
| Debt notice | הודעת חוב | You owe money, pay up | HIGH - interest accruing |
| Inquiry letter | מכתב בירורים | They want more information from you | MEDIUM - respond within stated deadline |
| Withholding certificate | אישור ניכוי מס במקור | Tax withholding rate for your business | LOW - informational |
| Fine notice | הודעת קנס | Penalty for late filing or payment | HIGH - pay or appeal within 30 days |

**What they usually want:** Money, documents proving your income/expenses, or for you to file returns you have not filed yet. The Tax Authority is the most deadline-sensitive body. Missing a 30-day window often means losing the right to object.

**Contact:** *4954 or 02-6559000, Sunday-Thursday 8:15-15:30. Online services at mof.gov.il.

## National Insurance Institute (המוסד לביטוח לאומי)

**Also known as:** Bituach Leumi, HaMossad LeBituach Leumi, NII

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- "המוסד לביטוח לאומי" with the Bituach Leumi logo (menorah-style symbol)
- Branch name (e.g., "סניף תל אביב", "סניף ירושלים")
- Division name for specific benefits

**Reference number patterns:**
- ID number based (9-digit teudat zehut)
- Claim number: "תביעה מס'" + number
- Payment reference: "אסמכתא" + number

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Benefit approval | אישור תביעה / אישור גמלה | Your claim was approved | LOW - good news |
| Benefit denial | דחיית תביעה | Your claim was denied | HIGH - 12 months to appeal |
| Partial approval | אישור חלקי | Approved but not full amount | MEDIUM - review and decide if to appeal |
| Reserve duty payment | אישור תגמולי מילואים | Your miluim compensation details | LOW - payment incoming |
| Debt collection | דרישת תשלום חוב | You owe them money (overpayment, missed payments) | HIGH - interest accruing |
| Disability percentage | קביעת אחוזי נכות | Medical committee decision on disability rating | HIGH if you disagree - appeal window |
| Child allowance update | עדכון קצבת ילדים | Change in your child benefit amount | LOW - informational |
| Maternity allowance | דמי לידה | Maternity/paternity benefit details | LOW - payment incoming |
| Unemployment benefits | דמי אבטלה | Unemployment benefit decision | MEDIUM - may require action |

**What they usually want:** To inform you of a decision, request additional medical documents, or notify you of a debt. Bituach Leumi letters are often dense with legal references but many are informational. The key question is always: "approved" or "denied."

**Contact:** *6050 or 02-6709090, Sunday-Thursday 8:00-17:00. Online at btl.gov.il.

## Municipalities (עיריות / מועצות מקומיות)

**Also known as:** Iriya, Moatza Mekomit, Local Authority

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- City/town emblem and name
- Department name: "אגף הגבייה" (collection), "מחלקת הארנונה" (arnona), "מחלקת רישוי" (licensing)

**Reference number patterns:**
- Property account number ("מס' חשבון נכס")
- Arnona account number
- Business license number

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Arnona bill | שובר ארנונה | Your property tax bill | HIGH - pay by due date |
| Arnona discount decision | החלטת ועדת הנחות | Decision on your discount request | MEDIUM - may need to reapply |
| Property reclassification | שינוי סיווג נכס | They changed your property classification (affects rate) | HIGH - 90 days to object |
| Building violation | הודעה על חריגת בנייה | They detected unauthorized construction | HIGH - legal consequences |
| Business license | רישיון עסק | Business license approval/renewal/denial | HIGH if denial |
| Water/sewage bill | חשבון מים/ביוב | Utility bill | MEDIUM - pay by due date |
| Zoning notice | הודעה על תוכנית בניין עיר | Zoning/planning change in your area | MEDIUM - right to object within stated period |
| Debt warning | התראה לפני נקיטת הליכים | Final warning before legal action | URGENT - act immediately |

**What they usually want:** Payment, compliance with building regulations, or to inform you of changes affecting your property. Municipalities are the most common source of "scary looking" letters that turn out to be routine bills.

**Contact:** Varies by municipality. Check your city's website for the relevant department.

## Interior Ministry (משרד הפנים)

**Also known as:** Misrad HaPnim, Population and Immigration Authority (רשות האוכלוסין וההגירה)

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- "משרד הפנים" or "רשות האוכלוסין וההגירה"
- May show specific bureau: "לשכת רישום" (registration office)

**Reference number patterns:**
- Application number ("בקשה מס'")
- Visa/permit number
- ID number (teudat zehut)

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Status decision | החלטה בעניין מעמד | Decision on residency/citizenship status | HIGH - appeal windows apply |
| Visa extension | הארכת אשרה | Visa extension approval or denial | HIGH - affects legal stay |
| ID card notice | הודעה בעניין תעודת זהות | ID card related (renewal, pickup) | MEDIUM |
| Passport notice | הודעה בעניין דרכון | Passport related | MEDIUM |
| Name change confirmation | אישור שינוי שם | Legal name change processed | LOW |
| Birth registration | רישום לידה | Birth registered | LOW |
| Marriage registration | רישום נישואין | Marriage registered | LOW |

**What they usually want:** Additional documents to process your application, notification of a decision, or for you to pick something up from their office.

## Land Registry (לשכת רישום המקרקעין / טאבו)

**Also known as:** Tabu, Land Registration Office

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- "לשכת רישום המקרקעין" under the Justice Ministry
- Regional office name

**Reference number patterns:**
- Block/Parcel numbers ("גוש" / "חלקה" / "תת-חלקה")
- Registration file number

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Registration confirmation | אישור רישום | Property transaction registered | LOW - good news |
| Lien notice | הודעת עיקול | A lien was placed on your property | URGENT |
| Caveat notice | הודעת הערת אזהרה | A caveat was registered on your property | HIGH - investigate |
| Correction notice | הודעת תיקון | Correction to registry records | MEDIUM |
| Missing documents | דרישת השלמת מסמכים | They need more documents to complete registration | MEDIUM |

**What they usually want:** Documents to complete a property registration, or to notify you of changes to your property's legal status.

## Courts (בתי משפט)

**Also known as:** Beit Mishpat, Beit Din (rabbinical)

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- State emblem + "בית משפט" + court type + location
- Court types: שלום (Magistrate), מחוזי (District), עליון (Supreme), תביעות קטנות (Small Claims), עבודה (Labor), משפחה (Family)

**Reference number patterns:**
- Case number format: ת"א (civil), ת"פ (criminal), ת"ק (small claims), תמ"ש (family)
- Year + sequential number (e.g., "ת"א 12345-01-26")

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Summons | הזמנה לדין | You are called to court (as plaintiff, defendant, or witness) | URGENT |
| Claim | כתב תביעה | Someone is suing you | URGENT - file defense |
| Judgment | פסק דין | Court's final decision | HIGH - appeal window |
| Interim order | צו ביניים | Temporary court order | URGENT - must comply |
| Hearing notice | הודעה על מועד דיון | Date for your next hearing | HIGH - must attend |
| Execution order | צו הוצאה לפועל | Court-ordered enforcement (debt collection) | URGENT |

**What they usually want:** Your appearance, your written response, or compliance with an order. Court documents are ALWAYS urgent and should be treated with maximum priority.

## Enforcement and Collection Authority (רשות האכיפה והגבייה / הוצאה לפועל)

**Also known as:** Hotzaa LaPoal, Execution Office

**Letterhead identifiers:**
- "רשות האכיפה והגבייה" or "לשכת ההוצאה לפועל"

**Reference number patterns:**
- Execution file number ("תיק הוצל"פ")

**Common document types:**

| Document | Hebrew Name | What It Means | Urgency |
|----------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Debt notice | אזהרה | You have an open enforcement file against you | URGENT - 20 days to respond |
| Property seizure | עיקול | Your assets/bank account has been frozen | URGENT |
| Payment arrangement | הסדר תשלומים | Terms for paying off debt | HIGH - comply or renegotiate |
| Restriction on leaving country | צו עיכוב יציאה מהארץ | You cannot leave Israel until debt resolved | URGENT |

**What they usually want:** Payment. Hotzaa LaPoal is the most aggressive collection mechanism in Israel. Their letters should always be treated as urgent. An "אזהרה" (warning) gives you 20 days to either pay, reach an arrangement, or file a claim of opposition ("התנגדות"). Ignoring it leads to asset seizure.

## Water Authority (רשות המים)

**Letterhead identifiers:** "רשות המים" under the Energy Ministry

**Common documents:** Rate notices, conservation notices, billing disputes. Generally low urgency unless it involves a fine.

---

## Electricity Authority (רשות החשמל)

**Letterhead identifiers:** "רשות החשמל" or from Israel Electric Corporation ("חברת החשמל")

**Common documents:** Rate changes, connection approvals, billing. IEC letters are usually bills or service notices.

---

## Ministry of Health (משרד הבריאות)

**Letterhead identifiers:** "משרד הבריאות" with the ministry emblem

**Common documents:** Business health permits, food establishment licenses, medical professional licensing, HMO-related notices.

---

## Israel Defense Forces (צה"ל)

**Letterhead identifiers:** IDF emblem, "צבא הגנה לישראל", specific unit or base name

**Common documents:** Reserve duty orders ("צו 8"), military records requests, benefits notices. Reserve duty orders are legally binding and have specific reporting dates.

## General Tips for Identifying Unknown Senders

1. **Check the return address** on the envelope, not just the letter
2. **Look for a "פרטי מענה"** (response details) section, which often names the department
3. **Google the phone number** on the letter to identify the office
4. **Reference number format** is often the strongest clue (each body uses distinct patterns)
5. **Legal citations** point to the administering body (e.g., "חוק הביטוח הלאומי" = Bituach Leumi)
6. **Collection notices** may come from private companies acting on behalf of a government body, always look for the original creditor name
