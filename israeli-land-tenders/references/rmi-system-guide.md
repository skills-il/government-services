# Israel Land Authority (RMI) System Guide

## Israeli Land System
- ~93% of land is state-owned (Israel Land Authority)
- ~7% privately owned (registered in Tabu)
- Leasehold model: 49-98 year leases
- 2009 reform: Allows conversion to ownership (residential)

## Affordable Housing Program: Dira BeHanacha
The affordable-housing lottery program, historically called "Mechir Lamishtaken"
(מחיר למשתכן), has been restructured as an umbrella program called "Dira
BeHanacha" (דירה בהנחה) with multiple sub-tracks. It is administered by the
Ministry of Construction and Housing (משרד הבינוי והשיכון), not by RMI directly.
The flow is two-step:

1. RMI tenders the land to developers under special conditions (reduced land price in exchange for below-market unit sale prices).
2. The Ministry of Construction and Housing runs eligibility (teudat zakaut), registration, and the random drawing for end buyers at `dira.moch.gov.il`.

Agents helping users "sign up for mechir lamishtaken" should direct them to
the Housing Ministry registration site, not the RMI tender portal.

### Dira BeHanacha sub-tracks (as of 2026)
| Sub-track | Hebrew | Discount cap | Grant | Audience |
|-----------|--------|--------------|-------|----------|
| General Mechir Lamishtaken | מחיר למשתכן כללי | no cap | varies by location | first-time buyers (chasrey dira) |
| Mehir Matara (Target Price) | מחיר מטרה | 20% off market or 500,000 NIS (lower of two) | 40,000 NIS uniform via mortgage bank | first-time buyers AND dwelling upgraders (mishaprey diur) |
| Mehir Muphchat (Reduced Price) | מחיר מופחת | varies | 40,000 NIS uniform | first-time buyers |
| Dira Lehaskir (Rent) | דירה להשכיר | long-term subsidized rental, not for sale | n/a | first-time buyers |

### Resale lockup
Cannot sell or rent the unit for **5 years from Form 4 (Tofes 4 / occupancy approval) OR 7 years from the lottery win date, whichever comes first**. Selling earlier triggers clawback of the discount.

### Eligibility certificate
- Application via the registration agents (Milgam, Alonim, Maof)
- Cost: around 200 NIS for the online application
- Required before registering for any lottery round

### Lottery 11 (April 2026)
- Registration opened: 15 April 2026
- Deadline for issuing eligibility certificates for this round: 30 April 2026
- Roughly 7,000 units across approximately 16 cities (Ashdod, Rehovot, Hadera, Ma'ale Adumim, Kiryat Gat, Nahariya, Eilat, Yehud, Rishon LeZion, Kfar Saba, Bet Dagan, Kadima-Tzoran, Rekhasim, Bnei Ayish, Kfar Manda, Bat Hefer, and more depending on source)
- Up to 50% of units reserved for active reservists (miluimnikim), about 25% of those for reserve combat soldiers
- Registrants can choose up to 3 cities and enter all lotteries in those cities
