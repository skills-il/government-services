# Filing checklist: each benefit → form → institution

Effective figures throughout: 01.01.2026. Verify the live form version before filing.

## Bituach Leumi (המוסד לביטוח לאומי)

| Benefit | Form | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Survivor's allowance (קצבת שאירים) | 410 (בל/410) | Also includes the death-grant and income-supplement claim. File online, at a branch, or by document upload. |
| Death grant (מענק פטירה) | 410 covers it | Usually paid automatically; separate claim only if not received. Amount 10,514 NIS. |
| Special survivors' allowance (גמלת שאירים מיוחדת) | 4506 | For non-insured olim survivors. |
| Orphan living allowance (דמי מחיה ליתומים) | 2910 | On top of the orphan allowance in defined cases. |
| Income supplement for old-age/survivors recipients (השלמת הכנסה) | 430 | Income-tested top-up. |
| She'erim / living-allowance income declaration | 412 | Income declaration used in the income test. |
| Family-status declaration and update | 408 | Report remarriage, new child, etc. Remarriage (or a new common-law relationship) TERMINATES the survivor's allowance and instead pays a one-time marriage grant (מענק נישואים) of 36 monthly allowances, in two installments (18 after the marriage, 18 about two years later). The allowance is CONTINUED if the new spouse is 60+ or cannot support himself with income below the 7,848 NIS ceiling, and REINSTATED if the widow(er) divorces or begins divorce proceedings within 10 years of remarrying. |
| Dependents' allowance, work-related death (קצבת תלויים) | 213 (בל/213) | File this INSTEAD of 410 when death resulted from a work injury. No income test; higher amounts. Cannot receive both tracks. |
| Burial fees (דמי קבורה) | No claim by the family | Paid directly from Bituach Leumi to the Chevra Kadisha / authorized burial body. Basic burial is free. |

## Pension fund (קרן פנסיה), separate from Bituach Leumi

| Benefit | Where to file | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Survivor's pension (פנסיית שאירים) | The deceased's pension fund directly | Typically ~60% of the deceased's salary/pension to the spouse for life, ~40% to orphans until 21 in a new comprehensive fund. Exact rates and eligible survivors are set by the fund's takanon, request the survivor-benefit application from the fund. Use "הר הביטוח" (Har HaBituach, Capital Market Authority, https://harb.cma.gov.il/) to locate all of the deceased's insurance policies and pension products, and "הר הכסף" (Har HaKesef, https://itur.mof.gov.il/) to trace lost or dormant pension savings, life-insurance policies, and bank accounts. |

## Provident fund / life insurance (קופת גמל, ביטוח חיים)

| Benefit | Where to file | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Beneficiary payout (מוטבים) | The provident fund manager / insurer directly | Under section 147 of the Inheritance Law these sums are outside the estate and go to the registered beneficiaries in the policy, overriding the will. If no beneficiary is registered, the money falls to the estate and needs a succession/probate order (out of scope, route to the wills/inheritance skill). |

## Adjacent benefits to check (mention + route)
- Study grant for single-parent families, bar/bat-mitzvah grant (Bituach Leumi, survivors section). The marriage grant on remarriage is covered in the form 408 row above.
- Vocational training + living allowance for widow(er)s (Bituach Leumi rehabilitation)
- Arnona (municipal property tax) discount and local-authority help → the local municipality
- Mourning leave and severance-to-survivors → the deceased's employer
