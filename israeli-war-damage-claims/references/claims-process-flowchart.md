# Property Damage Claims Process Flowchart

## Key Deadlines

| Deadline | Timeframe | From When |
|---|---|---|
| Initial damage notification | 14 days | Date of damage event |
| Full claim submission | 3 months | Date of damage event |
| Invoice submission after payment | 30 days | Date of receiving compensation payment |
| Appeal submission | Per decision letter | Date of receiving the decision |

## Process: Fast Track (מסלול מהיר)

Eligibility: Total claim up to NIS 30,000, individuals and businesses.

```
1. Damage occurs
   |
2. Document damage (photos, save evidence) -- within 14 days
   |
3. Open online claim at Tax Authority digital services
   |
4. Enter basic details (ID, address, damage description)
   |
5. Receive SMS with photo upload link
   |
6. Go to damage site, take photos FROM THAT LOCATION via the SMS link
   |
7. Upload photos + price quotes
   |
8. Submit declarations (affirm accuracy)
   |
9. Wait for approval (up to 7 days)
   |
10. Receive compensation payment
   |
11. Complete repairs
   |
12. Submit repair invoices within 30 days of payment
   |
   DONE
```

## Process: Regular Track (מסלול רגיל)

For claims exceeding NIS 30,000 or requiring professional assessment.

```
1. Damage occurs
   |
2. Document damage (photos, save evidence) -- within 14 days
   |
3. Open online claim at Tax Authority digital services
   |
4. Submit claim with documentation (photos, quotes)
   |
5. Claim acknowledged by Compensation Fund
   |
6. Assessor/appraiser (שמאי) visit scheduled
   |
7. Assessor inspects damage, prepares report
   |
8. Compensation amount determined
   |
   +--> If acceptable: proceed to step 9
   +--> If too low: file appeal (see Appeal Process below)
   |
9. Choose repair method:
   a. Self-repair (receive money, hire own contractors)
   b. Fund-authorized repair (fund arranges contractors)
   |
10. Receive compensation payment
   |
11. Complete repairs
   |
12. Submit repair invoices within 30 days of payment
   |
   DONE
```

## Process: Appeal (ערר)

```
1. Receive unsatisfactory decision (denial or low amount)
   |
2. Check deadline in decision letter
   |
3. Prepare written appeal with supporting evidence:
   - Independent repair quotes
   - Private assessor report (if applicable)
   - Photos and documentation
   - Explanation of why the decision is wrong
   |
4. Email appeal to: vaadat-erar@taxes.gov.il
   |
5. Compensation Fund submits response (within 60 days)
   |
6. Appeal committee (ועדת ערר) hearing
   |
7. Decision in writing within 30 days of hearing
   |
   +--> If acceptable: receive adjusted compensation
   +--> If not: request permission to appeal to District Court
```

## Who Can File

| Person | Can File For |
|---|---|
| Property owner | Structure + contents |
| Tenant / renter | Contents they own |
| Person bearing restoration costs | Structure (even if not owner) |
| Business owner | Business property + equipment + inventory |
