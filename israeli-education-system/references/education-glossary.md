## Bagrut Mandatory Subjects
- Hebrew Language (Lashon): 2 units min
- English (Anglit): 3-5 units
- Mathematics (Matematika): 3-5 units
- Bible (Tanach): 2 units
- Literature (Sifrut): 2 units
- History (Historia): 2 units
- Civics (Ezrachut): 2 units
- Minimum 21 total units for certificate
