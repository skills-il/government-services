# Israeli Healthcare System Overview

## National Health Insurance Law (1995)
- Universal coverage for all Israeli residents
- Funded by health tax via Bituach Leumi
- Four competing health funds (kupot cholim)
- Government-defined health basket (sal briut)

## Four Health Funds

| Fund | Hebrew | Members | Market Share |
|------|--------|---------|-------------|
| Clalit | clalit sherutei briut | ~4.8M | ~52% |
| Maccabi | maccabi sherutei briut | ~2.5M | ~27% |
| Meuhedet | kupat cholim meuhedet | ~1.3M | ~14% |
| Leumit | kupat cholim leumit | ~0.7M | ~7% |

## Hospital Quality Indicators
Published quarterly by Ministry of Health:
- Standardized mortality ratio
- Surgical site infection rate
- Central line infection rate
- 30-day readmission rate
- Emergency department wait time
- Patient satisfaction
- Bed occupancy rate

## Patient Rights
1. Choice of health fund
2. Health basket coverage
3. Maximum wait times
4. Second medical opinion
5. Access to medical records
6. Informed consent
7. Privacy
8. Annual fund switching
9. Complaint/ombudsman process
